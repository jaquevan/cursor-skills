# Humanized Text

Hey — wanted to give you a heads up on the prototype-creator eval framework I've been working on. It uses Playwright-based persona walkthroughs to test prototypes against their Jira acceptance criteria. It covers seven usability dimensions and spits out an HTML report at the end.

Happy to walk you through the technical side whenever works for you. I think it could really streamline how we evaluate prototypes. Let me know if you have any questions!

**Changed:** Removed corporate filler ("I wanted to reach out," "it's important to note," "don't hesitate to reach out"), replaced stiff phrasing ("delve into," "comprehensively assess," "truly transformative," "robust") with conversational language, cut the em dash in favor of simpler sentence flow, and tightened the overall structure so it reads like something you'd actually send on Slack.
