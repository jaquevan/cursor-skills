# Humanize Text – Eval 3 (Without Skill)

## Original

"I would like to express my interest in contributing to the working group. I have extensive experience with evaluation frameworks and would be delighted to share my insights. Furthermore, I believe my background in user experience research — combined with my technical skills — would make me a valuable addition to the team."

## Rewritten

Hey! I'd love to contribute to the working group. I've spent a lot of time working with evaluation frameworks and would be happy to share what I've learned. I also have a background in UX research and a solid technical side, so I think I could bring a lot to the table.

## Changed:
- "I would like to express my interest" → "I'd love to" — more direct and enthusiastic
- "extensive experience" → "spent a lot of time working with" — less résumé-speak
- "would be delighted to share my insights" → "would be happy to share what I've learned" — simpler and warmer
- "Furthermore, I believe" → dropped entirely — unnecessary filler
- "user experience research — combined with my technical skills — would make me a valuable addition" → "background in UX research and a solid technical side, so I think I could bring a lot to the table" — conversational phrasing instead of formal self-assessment
- Added "Hey!" opener for a casual tone
